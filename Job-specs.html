Web Development Job Specifications

Frontend Developer

Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Experience with frontend frameworks such as React, Vue.js, or Angular.

Ability to convert design mockups into responsive web pages.

Understanding of cross-browser compatibility issues and ways to work around them.

Creativity in UI/UX design to enhance user experience.

Backend Developer

Strong understanding of server-side languages such as Node.js, Python, or Ruby.

Experience with databases like MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL.

Knowledge of RESTful APIs and web services.

Ability to write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.

Problem-solving skills to tackle complex backend challenges.

Full Stack Developer

Combination of skills from both frontend and backend development.

Ability to work on both client-side and server-side programming.

Experience with version control systems such as Git.

Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Passion for learning new technologies and adapting to changes in the industry.
